◆ What is Tree of Life by Taj?

Tree of Life by Taj is the boutique & leisure resort brand under the Taj Hotels portfolio — designed to offer intimate, nature-immersive luxury experiences in India's most coveted destinations.

Unlike large city hotels, Tree of Life properties are curated escapes — each with 15–30 rooms, thoughtfully designed architecture, organic F&B, and personalised service. The Alibag property sits on the coveted Konkan coast, one of Maharashtra's fastest-growing luxury leisure corridors.

Managed end-to-end by Taj's operations team, your villa will benefit from Taj's pan-India & international marketing reach, its loyalty programme (Taj InnerCircle with 4M+ members), and its proven revenue management systems — ensuring consistently high occupancy and returns.
